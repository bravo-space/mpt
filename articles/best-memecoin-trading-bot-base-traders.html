Base setup checklist before you trade

1. Use a dedicated trading wallet

Keep active memecoin trading funds separate from long-term holdings and separate from DeFi activity.

2. Check liquidity before sizing up

Do not trust movement alone. Check whether the token is actually tradeable at your size.

3. Review holder concentration and wallet behavior

Look for unstable structure before entry, especially linked wallets, heavy concentration, or suspicious launch behavior.

4. Check contract and launch quality

A fast entry into a weak contract is still a bad trade.

5. Set execution rules before entry

Define slippage, max size, invalidation, stop loss, and take profit before buying.

6. Track the right Base venues

Focus on where relevant Base activity actually happens, then act selectively when the setup deserves it.
